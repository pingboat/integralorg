Where traditional wisdom meets modern innovation. We experiment, prototype, and test new approaches to creating dignified rural livelihoods.

What is Reality Lab?

Reality Lab is our experimental space where we develop and test new ideas before scaling them across communities. We believe that real innovation happens when you combine traditional knowledge with modern tools and methods.

Here, we prototype new products, test farming techniques, experiment with market approaches, and develop training programs—all in partnership with the communities we serve.

Lab Visual

Focus Areas

Our experiments span multiple domains, all aimed at improving livelihoods for rural communities.

Product Innovation

Developing new silk products, natural dyes, and value-added offerings that meet modern market demands while preserving traditional techniques.

Agricultural Techniques

Testing sustainable farming methods, integrated pest management, and climate-resilient practices for silk and host plant cultivation.

Technology Integration

Exploring how digital tools can improve traceability, connect artisans to markets, and streamline operations without losing the human touch.
