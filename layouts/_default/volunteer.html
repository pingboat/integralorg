Become a Volunteer

Join our community of changemakers. Whether you can spare a few hours or a few months, your skills and passion can help transform lives in North East India.

Your Time Can Create Real Impact

At Integral Foundation, volunteers are the heartbeat of our mission. From skilled professionals sharing expertise remotely to passionate individuals working alongside our team on the ground — every contribution matters.

We believe in meaningful volunteering that benefits both the communities we serve and the volunteers themselves. You'll gain unique experiences, build lasting connections, and see firsthand how sustainable development transforms lives.

On-Ground (Short-term)

  • Artisan training assistance

Fellowship Program

A deeper commitment for those ready to drive significant impact.

  • Lead specific projects or initiatives
  • Mentorship from our leadership team
  • Stipend provided
  • Housing support in Assam
  • Certificate upon completion
3-6 months
